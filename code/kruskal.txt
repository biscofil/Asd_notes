Kruskal(G,w)
     A = $\emptyset$
     Foreach u in V[G] do                             	$O(n)$
         Make_set(u)
     Ordina gli archi di E[G] in ordine crescente     	$\Theta(m \log m)$
     Foreach (u,v) in E[G] do                         	$O(m \log m)$
         If find_set(u) != find_set(v) then           	$O(\log m)$*
	        Union(u,v)                              $O(\log m)$*
              	A = $A \cup \{(u,v)\}$
     Return A
